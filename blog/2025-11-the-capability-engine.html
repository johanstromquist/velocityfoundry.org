"Leadership doesn't value HR transformation."

"We're too small/specialized for this."

"But what about compliance?"
Embed it. Don't enforce it. Build rules into systems so violations become impossible. Automated alerts for certifications expiring. Workflows that won't proceed without required approvals. Compliance becomes architecture, not policing.
